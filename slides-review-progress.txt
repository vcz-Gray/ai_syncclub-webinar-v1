## Codebase Patterns
- Do not change the slide canvas size; only adjust slide-local layout.
- Image-heavy slides need their own classes and should not inherit generic text-first spacing rules.
- Browser measurement using `.slide.scrollHeight` vs `.slide.clientHeight` is the fastest way to catch projection overflow.
- Keep the local source HTML and `webinar/index.html` synchronized in every iteration.
